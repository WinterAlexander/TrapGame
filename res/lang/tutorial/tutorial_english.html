How to play

  1. Click a  to make it yours ()
  2. Then, click an adjacent  to expand your area.
  3. If you draw a shape with your color, it will be filled automatically.
  4. Be the fastest and trap your opponents around your color to seize victory.
  5. Have fun ! :^)

Tips & Strategy

  • As with mouse buttons, Space bar and keys W & E can be used to click on buttons.
  • Auto-filling only works if all players started playing.
  • Do not start games in center ! You will be surrounded from all sides. A better strategy is to start from one side and go to the middle.

Commands (advanced)

  • Some commands are available in games. They can kick a player with a bad behavior, change the board size or even show a player's statistics.
  • To show the list of commands in your game chat, type "/list". The list will be shown only for you.
  • To find how a command works and what's it's purpose, type "/help command" replacing "command" by your actual command.
  • To get access to the most important commands, you need to be a super user. If you host a game, you will be the super user of your game.
  • If you are a super user, you can share your permissions with other players with the command "/superuser player" where player is the player you want to give permissions.
