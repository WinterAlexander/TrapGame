How to play

  1. Click a  to make it yours 
  2. Then, click an adjacent  to expand your area.
  3. Be the fastest and trap your opponents around your color to seize victory.
  4. Have fun ! :^)

Commands

  • When you are joined in, you can use commands to interact with the game
  • Type /list in chat to get the list of commands and /help to get help about one
  • Commands are powerful, you can change the board size, kick another player or consult statistics
  • Some important commands require to be a super user, please do not abuse of theses commands
